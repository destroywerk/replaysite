Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add an RSS feed?

Open Replay on Android and tap the Feeds tab at the bottom. Tap the + button in the top right corner. Paste the RSS or Atom feed URL (e.g. https://example.com/feed.xml) and tap Add Feed. The feed will be fetched and added to your list immediately.

How does Chrome sync work?

The Replay Chrome extension lets you save any web page to your Replay reading queue with one click. Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store, sign in with the same Google account you use in the Android app, and tap the Replay icon in your Chrome toolbar while on any page you want to save. It will appear in your Saved tab in the Android app within seconds.

How do I sign in?

Replay uses Google Sign-In for authentication. Open the app and tap Sign in with Google. Select your Google account, grant the necessary permissions, and you'll be signed in. Use the same Google account on both the Android app and Chrome extension to keep everything in sync.

My saved articles aren't syncing — what should I do?

First, check that you're signed in to the same Google account in both the Chrome extension and the Android app. Then check your internet connection. If the issue persists, try signing out and back in on both platforms. If it's still not working, contact us with details of your device and Android version.

Can I use Replay without signing in?

You can browse RSS feeds without signing in. However, signing in is required to save articles to your reading queue and to use the Chrome extension sync feature.
