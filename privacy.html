Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026

Replay ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Replay Android app and Chrome extension.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the Replay service:

  • Google account email address — collected when you sign in with Google. Used solely to identify your account and sync your data across devices.
  • Article URLs and titles — URLs of articles you save or bookmark within the app or via the Chrome extension. This data is tied to your account to enable cross-device sync.
  • RSS feed URLs — the feed URLs you subscribe to within the app, stored to restore your subscriptions across devices.

We do not collect browsing history beyond articles you explicitly save, and we do not collect any payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to provide the Replay service:

  • To sync your saved articles, reading queue, and RSS subscriptions across your Android device and Chrome extension.
  • To authenticate you via Google Sign-In and associate your data with your account.
  • To restore your data if you reinstall the app or sign in on a new device.

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose beyond providing the Replay service.

3. Third-Party Services

Replay uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Firebase / Cloud Firestore — used to store and sync your saved articles, reading queue, and feed subscriptions. Data is stored in Google's secure cloud infrastructure. See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sign-In — used for authentication. We receive your Google account email address and a unique identifier. See Google Privacy Policy.

We do not use any advertising networks, analytics services that track personal behaviour, or other third-party data processors.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with the third-party services listed above as necessary to operate the app.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at contact@replay-app.com.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and Firebase's built-in security rules, to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Replay is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at contact@replay-app.com.
