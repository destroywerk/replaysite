The Workflow

From browser
to reading queue
in one click.

The Replay Chrome extension bridges your desktop browsing and your Android reading list — so nothing interesting slips through the cracks.

1

Install the Chrome extension

Add the Replay extension to Chrome and sign in with your account. It lives quietly in your toolbar until you need it.

2

Save any page with one click

On any article or webpage you want to read later, click the Replay icon. It's saved instantly — title, URL, and all.

3

Read on Android, anywhere

Open Replay on your phone and your saved articles are already there, synced and ready for your commute or couch.
